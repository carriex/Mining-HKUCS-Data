What We Do

Each year, HKU Computer Science Department attracts hundreds of applications for doing a graduate study. From these applications, numerous data are collected, including student profiles, scholarships, teachers’ comments and admission decisions. How can we make use of these data to refine our admission strategies and facilitate the admission process?
Data Extraction

Automatically extract key information from students’ application forms and documents.

Data Visualization

Create visual representation of data and make complex data more understandable, accessible and usable.

Pattern Analysis

Provide a data mining tool box to perform advanced data analytics, unravel patterns and better understand trends.

Smart Filtering

Filter students’ applications according to user-defined requirements and criteria.

Intelligent Matching

Suggest appropriate students to teachers based on their interest.

Outcome Prediction

Predict student's application outcome and make implications on the admission strategies of HKUCS.
